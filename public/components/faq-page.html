Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a Streak?

A streak counts the number of consecutive days you mark at least one task as "Done" (green checkmark).

  • Mark today's task as Done to keep your streak alive.
  • If you miss a day (no task marked Done), your streak resets to 0.
  • Your Longest Streak is tracked automatically and shown on the Streak Card.
  • Achievements unlock at 7-day and 30-day streaks.

Tip: Even on busy days, marking one small task as Done keeps your streak going!

What are Achievements and how do I unlock them?

Achievements are badges that unlock automatically when you reach specific milestones:

  • First Step — Complete your first day's task.
  • 7-Day Streak — Study 7 days in a row.
  • 30-Day Streak — Study 30 days in a row.
  • Arithmetic Master — Complete 80% of Arithmetic topics.
  • DI Master — Complete 80% of Data Interpretation topics.
  • File Collector — Solve 100 practice files.
  • Video Learner — Watch 50 videos.
  • Mock Warrior — Take 10 mock tests.

You don't need to manually claim them — they activate automatically once the condition is met, and the date is recorded.

How does Subject Progress work?

Your course plan is divided into 5 phases, each representing a major area of study:

  • Fundamentals — Number System, Simplification, Quadratic Equations, etc.
  • Data Interpretation — Prelims DI, Caselet DI.
  • Arithmetic — Percentage, Ratio, Profit & Loss, Time & Work, etc.
  • Speed & Distance — Trains, Boats, Time-Speed-Distance.
  • Advanced — Mensuration, P&C, Probability, Final Revisions.

The Subject Progress section shows a progress bar for each phase. The percentage is calculated as (tasks Done / total tasks) in that phase. As you mark tasks complete, the bars fill up automatically.

How does the Telegram Bot help me?

The Telegram Bot keeps you accountable with automated reminders and progress updates:

  • Daily Reminders — Get a morning reminder with today's topic, videos, and files.
  • Evening Check-in — A nudge in the evening to mark your task as Done.
  • Missed Alerts — If you haven't marked a task by night, you get a "you missed today" alert.
  • Progress Updates — Weekly summary of your completion rate and streak.
  • OTP Authentication — The bot sends a 6-digit OTP to verify your Telegram account during registration.

To get started: open the bot on Telegram, type /start, copy your numeric Telegram User ID, and paste it during registration. All reminders use Indian Standard Time (IST).

How do I use the Course Selector and Video Tracker?

Next to the Course Plan heading, you'll find a searchable dropdown to select subjects:

  • Quant, Reasoning, English, General Studies — Built-in subjects with dummy data.
  • Custom — Add your own subject with custom topics and videos.
  • Click the pin icon on a pinned subject to open the Video Tracker.
  • In the Video Tracker, add videos with title, duration, mock test status, revisions, and mock attempts.

Double-tap a topic name to shift the schedule -1 day. Triple-tap to shift +1 day.

Can I change the theme?

Yes! Click the Theme button in the header to open the theme picker. You can preview themes before saving. Your choice is saved automatically and synced to the cloud when logged in.
